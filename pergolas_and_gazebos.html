PERGOLAS

  • A pergola is a freestanding structure open to sky, forming a shaded walkway, passageway, or sitting area of vertical posts or pillars that usually support cross-beams and a sturdy open lattice.Pergolas may link pavilions or extend from a building's door to an open garden feature such as an isolated terrace or pool.

  • Freestanding pergolas alongwith the combination of louvers- provide a sitting area that allows for breeze and light sun but offer protection from the harsh glare of direct sunlight.We can also create multiple patterns and partitions with the help of different Pergola's elements.

  • We are having a complete range of different pergolas to explore- ranging from Treated-Wood, FRP, Solar, to Retractable Aluminium Pergolas.

GAZEBOS

  • Gazebos are also freestanding or attached to a garden wall structure, though unlike pergolas they are roofed, and open on all sides.

  • They provide shade, shelter from rain and a place to rest, while acting as an ornamental feature.Some gazebos in public parks are large enough to serve as a bandstand.

  • With us, you can choose from a variety of roofing to be provided on top, structure can be done with different materials as per client's budget and choices.
